Social Hero

Mini project at University of California, Santa Cruz

This was a mini project during 'Intro to Design Methods' class at UCSC

Project length: ~2 weeks

Team members: 5 (Franky Dominguez, Naomi Du, Sakshi Shah, Siddharth Subramanian, Yi Qing Khoo(me))

Task: Design something based on a selected community (Community with social anxiety/would like to build social skills)

Results: A mobile game called Social Hero that helps to foster social courage through daily challenges and in-app social interactions.

My main role: 1 out 3 members in-charge of prototyping/design.

My Accomplishments

  • Created multiple prototypes ranging from low fidelity sketches to high fidelity interactable figma prototypes
  • Conducted a few user testing with classmates to identify potential pain points of the then design
  • Looked into similar apps currently available in app stores as part of competitor analysis

Challenges Faced

Through the user interviews conducted by the team, we came to realise that interviewees were unfamiliar with the concept of how gamification can help with social anxiety.

Further work on...

To further work on this game, I think it would be great to talk to a professional regarding the type of quests users should complete to allow them to foster social courage. It would also be great to conduct more stakeholder interviewers to understand more needs of the community as given the time constraint, our team was only able to interview schoolmates.
